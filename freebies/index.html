PVTrace is become open-source for the benefit of the PV community! PVTrace can be licensed for commercial work or consulting projects. Become a developer soon...

Quantum of Sol: Now a Webapp!

Quantum of Sol was our highly successful outreach adventure at prestigious Royal Summer Science Exhibition 2009. First year summer project students Matthias Kraft and Aidan Taylor joined the research group to make a webapp that describes the losses in a Shockley-Queisser solar and how they can be overcome by moving to advanced PV concepts such at hot-carriers.

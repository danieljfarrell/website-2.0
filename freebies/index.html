About

Daniel is presently a post doctoral researcher in the Experimental Solid State Physics group at Imperial College London. His research activities revolve around approaches to increase the efficiency and reduce the cost of solar energy conversion and include: hot-carrier solar cells and spectral modification with luminescent materials.
